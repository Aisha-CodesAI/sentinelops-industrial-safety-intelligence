## Overview

SentinelOps is an AI-powered Industrial Safety Intelligence platform designed to prevent workplace incidents before they occur. The platform acts as an intelligence layer between industrial safety systems, correlating data from sensors, permits, worker activity, compliance records, and historical incidents to identify compound risks that traditional monitoring systems often miss.

Rather than reacting to alarms after thresholds are breached, SentinelOps predicts dangerous conditions, provides actionable recommendations, and orchestrates emergency response workflows in real time.

---

## Problem Statement

India's heavy industrial sector continues to experience thousands of workplace incidents despite widespread deployment of:

- IoT Sensors
- SCADA Systems
- CCTV Monitoring
- Permit-to-Work Systems
- Maintenance Tracking Platforms

These systems often operate independently, resulting in:

- Missed correlations between critical events
- Delayed operational decisions
- Increased risk of severe incidents
- Reactive rather than proactive safety management

SentinelOps addresses this challenge by creating a unified AI-driven safety intelligence layer.
